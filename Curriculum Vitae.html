Hi, I’m Jae Lee

Teacher of English to Speakers of Other Languages,

English Learning Coordinator

Education is everywhere all over the world and will be always in the future. Although we all are living with Education every learner has different perspectives, motivations, and outcomes. Teaching and learning a language subjects to the understanding of diverse cultures and individuals. The last twenty-seven years of my life seem to have strangely but purposely unfolded to where it is now – a point of delving into an exciting endeavor of being a teacher of English to speakers of other languages. Diverse experiences in many different places such as South Korea, England, Japan, and Thailand allowed me to have various perspectives in Education, more specifically in learning English as a second language. Whom you are learning from is a great successful example of an English learner as a second language. I chose to achieve my bachelor’s degree in English, Language Arts Education to deeply understand the language, English. Now, I am pursuing MA in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages program to have a solid perception and a professional viewpoint as an Educator.
